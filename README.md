
# Medical Database Project

An oracle database with tables for Patients, Doctors, Bills, Visits, and Diagnoses.
```db_project.pdf``` contains an ER diagram and schema tables.



### Queries

1.  Total visits per doctor

2.  Top 5 patients by total bill amount

3.  Most common diagnosis

4.  Visits per city

5.  Average bill per doctor

6.  Patients who had unpaid bills

7.  Doctors with patients diagnosed with Diabetes

8.  Average total bill amount by payment status

9.  List visits and their diagnoses

10. Top doctors by total billed revenue – Rank doctors by total revenue generated.
